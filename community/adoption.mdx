## Adoption

If you're using Serverless Workflow in your projects and would like to showcase your adoption, become an Adopter! By joining our community of adopters, you'll have the opportunity to share your experiences, contribute feedback, and collaborate with like-minded individuals and organizations leveraging Serverless Workflow to power their workflows.

### Benefits of Being an Adopter

As an official adopter, you'll receive:

- **Visibility**: Your organization will be listed on our website and documentation as a Serverless Workflow adopter
- **Community Recognition**: Showcase your commitment to open-source and cloud-native technologies
- **Networking**: Connect with other adopters and share best practices
- **Influence**: Provide feedback that helps shape the future of the specification
- **Early Access**: Stay informed about upcoming features and changes
- **Support**: Get prioritized community support and guidance

### How to Become an Adopter

To officially become a Serverless Workflow adopter:

1. **Submit Your Adoption Story**
   - Open a pull request to add your organization to the adopters list
   - Include your organization name, logo, and a brief description of how you use Serverless Workflow
   - Share your use case and implementation details (optional but encouraged)

2. **Share Your Experience**
   - Write a blog post about your implementation
   - Present at our [weekly meetings](/community/meetings)
   - Share your story on social media with #ServerlessWorkflow

3. **Engage with the Community**
   - Participate in [discussions](https://github.com/serverlessworkflow/specification/discussions)
   - Provide feedback on proposals and specifications
   - Help answer questions from other users

### Adoption Use Cases

We're interested in all types of Serverless Workflow implementations:

- **Production Workloads**: Critical business processes and workflows
- **Developer Tools**: Integration into CI/CD pipelines and automation
- **Data Processing**: ETL, data pipelines, and analytics workflows
- **Event-Driven Architectures**: Event processing and orchestration
- **Microservices Orchestration**: Service coordination and choreography
- **IoT and Edge Computing**: Distributed workflow execution

## Sponsoring

As an open-source project, Serverless Workflow relies on the support of sponsors to sustain its development and growth.

By becoming a sponsor, you'll not only demonstrate your commitment to advancing serverless technologies but also gain visibility within our vibrant community.

### Why Sponsor Serverless Workflow?

- **Support Open Source**: Help maintain and improve a critical cloud-native specification
- **Industry Leadership**: Demonstrate your organization's commitment to serverless and workflow technologies
- **Community Visibility**: Gain recognition within the CNCF and serverless communities
- **Influence Direction**: Have a voice in the future development of the specification
- **Attract Talent**: Show potential employees your commitment to open-source technologies
- **Give Back**: Support the tools and standards your organization depends on

### Sponsorship Levels

Sponsorship opportunities range from financial contributions to in-kind support:

#### Financial Sponsorship

- Direct funding for project development
- Support for community events and meetings
- Infrastructure and tooling costs
- Documentation and marketing efforts

**[Become a Sponsor](https://crowdfunding.lfx.linuxfoundation.org/projects/serverless-workflow)**

#### In-Kind Sponsorship

- Developer time for specification work
- Infrastructure and hosting services
- Testing and quality assurance resources
- Documentation and technical writing
- Event space and logistics support
- Marketing and community outreach

### How Sponsorship Funds Are Used

Your sponsorship directly supports:

- **Development**: Core specification development and maintenance
- **Tooling**: SDKs, runtimes, and developer tools
- **Documentation**: High-quality guides, tutorials, and examples
- **Testing**: Conformance testing and quality assurance
- **Community**: Events, meetings, and community building
- **Outreach**: Conference presentations and marketing materials

### Become a Sponsor

To sponsor Serverless Workflow:

1. **Financial Sponsorship**: Visit our [LFX Crowdfunding page](https://crowdfunding.lfx.linuxfoundation.org/projects/serverless-workflow)

2. **In-Kind Sponsorship**: Contact the maintainers at [cncf-serverlessws-maintainers@lists.cncf.io](mailto:cncf-serverlessws-maintainers@lists.cncf.io)

3. **Corporate Membership**: Consider joining the [CNCF](https://www.cncf.io/about/join/) to support all CNCF projects including Serverless Workflow
