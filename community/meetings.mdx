## Weekly Meetings

The Serverless Workflow team meets weekly to discuss project development, review contributions, and collaborate on the future of the specification.

### Meeting Schedule

- **Frequency**: Weekly
- **Day**: Every Thursday
- **Time**: 9:00 AM ET (USA Eastern Time)
- **Duration**: 1 hour

### How to Join

To register for meetings, please visit the [CNCF Community Calendar](https://tockify.com/cncf.public.events/monthly?search=serverless%20workflow).

You can register for:
- Individual meetings
- The entire meeting series

Once registered, you will receive calendar invitations with meeting links and connection details.

### Meeting Agenda

Typical meeting topics include:

- Specification updates and proposals
- Pull request reviews and discussions
- Community contributions and feedback
- SDK and runtime updates
- Ecosystem tool announcements
- Use case presentations
- Q&A sessions

### Meeting Notes and Recordings

Meeting notes and recordings are typically shared with the community after each meeting. Check the following resources:

- [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/serverlessworkflow/specification/discussions) for meeting notes
- Community Slack channel (#serverless-workflow) for announcements
- CNCF YouTube channel for meeting recordings

## Other Ways to Participate

If you cannot attend the weekly meetings, you can still stay involved:

### Asynchronous Communication

- **Slack**: Join the [CNCF Slack](https://slack.cncf.io/) #serverless-workflow channel
- **GitHub Discussions**: Participate in [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/serverlessworkflow/specification/discussions)
- **GitHub Issues**: Comment on [issues](https://github.com/serverlessworkflow/specification/issues) and pull requests

## Meeting Etiquette

To ensure productive meetings, please:

- Join on time or slightly early
- Mute your microphone when not speaking
- Use the "raise hand" feature to request speaking time
- Be respectful of other participants and their viewpoints
- Stay on topic and follow the agenda
- Follow the [Code of Conduct](/community/code-of-conduct)

## Proposing Meeting Topics

If you'd like to propose a topic for discussion at a weekly meeting:

1. Open a [GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/serverlessworkflow/specification/discussions) or [issue](https://github.com/serverlessworkflow/specification/issues)
2. Post in the #serverless-workflow Slack channel
3. Email the maintainers at [cncf-serverlessws-maintainers@lists.cncf.io](mailto:cncf-serverlessws-maintainers@lists.cncf.io)

We welcome all relevant topics and community input!
